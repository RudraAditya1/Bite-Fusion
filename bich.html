details

Bihari Chicken is a traditional dish from the state of Bihar in India, known for its rich and flavorful preparation. It typically involves marinating chicken pieces in a mixture of yogurt and spices, including ginger, garlic, turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is then cooked slowly over low heat to allow the flavors to meld together and the chicken to become tender. One distinctive feature of Bihari Chicken is the use of mustard oil, which adds a unique and pungent flavor to the dish. The dish is often garnished with fresh cilantro and served with rice or Indian bread (roti or paratha). Bihari Chicken is a favorite among meat lovers in Bihar and is often served at special occasions and gatherings. It is loved for its bold flavors and aromatic spices, making it a must-try for anyone interested in the rich culinary traditions of Bihar.
